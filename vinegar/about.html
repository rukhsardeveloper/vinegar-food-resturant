    The Aroma That Started It All

    Vinegar Food Restaurant, the flagship brand of JADONS Ltd, began its journey in 1994 with a single restaurant and an audacious dream — to spread its unique culinary aroma across the country. Known as "The Serving Plates," our brand name has become synonymous with quality, warmth, and excellence.

    Over three decades, we have grown from a humble kitchen to a national institution. Today, with over 1,000 branches nationwide, 72 rotating menus that change with the seasons, and a staggering 240,000+ unique delicacies, Vinegar continues to redefine the art of dining in this country.

    • 🌟 Founded in 1994 by JADONS Ltd with a vision for culinary excellence
    • 🗺️ 1,000+ branches spanning every major city and town
    • 🔄 72 rotating menus updated weekly with seasonal inspirations
    • 🍜 240,000+ unique delicacies catalogued in our culinary database
    • 👨‍🍳 Over 15,000 expert chefs trained in our culinary academies
    • 🏆 Multiple national and international culinary awards
