About Vinegar

"The Serving Plates" – A Legacy of Flavor

Vinegar Food Restaurant, the flagship brand of JADONS Ltd, has spread its aroma across the culinary world for decades. Known as "the Serving plates," we have grown from a single kitchen to one of the most celebrated restaurant chains in the country.

  • 🏆 Multiple national and international culinary awards
  • 🗺️ 1,000+ branches spread across the nation
  • 🍜 240,000+ unique delicacies on our exclusive database
  • 🤝 Corporate & private catering with bespoke menus
